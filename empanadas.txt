1 - package Goya dough disks
1lb. - ground meat
1 - pepper
1 - small onion
salt - to taste
black pepper - to taste

1. Finely dice pepper and onion
2. In sautee pan, over medium heat, begin cooking pepper and onion
3. Once onion is translucent, add ground meat to brown, then drain
4. Using one disk, add 1 - 2 tablespoons of meat mixture fold in half
and with a fork press the edge closed.
5. Set aside and repeat with remaining disks and mixture
6. Heat oil to 350 degrees
7. Add 1 - 2 empanadas to hot oil
8. After 3 min turn over and allow to cook for another 3 min
9. Remove from oil to paper towel lined plate to drain
